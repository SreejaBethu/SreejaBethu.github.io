Travel Booking Platform Redesign

Revamping an existing travel platform to enhance usability and customer satisfaction.

Overview

This project focused on redesigning a desktop and mobile travel booking platform to improve its user experience, streamline the booking flow, and increase conversion rates. Key areas of focus included search functionality, itinerary management, and personalized recommendations.

Role: UX Designer, Interaction Designer

Tools: Figma, Miro, UsabilityHub

Timeline: 8 weeks

Problem Statement

The existing travel booking platform suffered from:

  • A cluttered interface leading to cognitive overload.
  • Confusing search filters and results display.
  • Poor mobile responsiveness, hindering on-the-go booking.
  • Lack of engaging content and personalized travel inspirations.

My Process

My approach integrated agile methodologies with a strong emphasis on continuous user feedback:

Empathize

Surveys, user interviews, journey mapping.

Ideate

Brainstorming, sketching, concept development.

Prototype

Low-to-high fidelity prototyping for various scenarios.

Test & Iterate

Usability testing, A/B testing, data analysis.

Research & Discovery

User Surveys & Interviews

Gathered insights from frequent travelers on their current frustrations and desires for an ideal booking experience. A key finding was the overwhelming number of options and confusing jargon.

Competitive Benchmarking

Analyzed top travel sites (e.g., Expedia, Booking.com, Kayak) to identify successful patterns and gaps in the market that our redesign could address.

Design Solutions & Iterations

Streamlined Search & Filtering

Introduced a more intuitive search interface with smart defaults and progressive disclosure for advanced filters, making it easier for users to find what they need without being overwhelmed.

Enhanced Itinerary Management

Created a centralized "My Trips" dashboard where users could easily view, manage, and modify all aspects of their bookings from one place.

Personalized Recommendations

Integrated a lightweight recommendation engine to suggest destinations, accommodations, and activities based on user preferences and past booking history.

Usability Testing & Refinements

Conducted two rounds of usability testing with diverse users. Early prototypes were tested for flow and basic usability, while later high-fidelity designs focused on visual appeal and microinteractions. Key refinements included:

  • Revising the date picker for better mobile usability.
  • Improving error message clarity during checkout.
  • Adjusting button hierarchy for clearer call-to-actions.

Impact & Outcomes

Key Achievements:

  • User testing showed a **25% reduction in task completion time** for primary booking flows.
  • Initial feedback indicated a **significant increase in perceived ease of use** and visual appeal.
  • The new design laid the groundwork for future feature expansions, such as group booking and loyalty programs.

The redesign successfully addressed the initial pain points, resulting in a more user-friendly, efficient, and visually appealing travel booking platform.
