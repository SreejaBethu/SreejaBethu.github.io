About Me

I am a data-driven and results-oriented analyst with a unique blend of expertise across Data, Business, and Product domains. My passion lies in transforming raw data into actionable insights, bridging the gap between technical details and strategic business objectives, and guiding product development with a clear understanding of user behavior and market needs.

My journey is defined by a commitment to critical thinking and problem-solving. I thrive in environments where I can leverage analytical tools and methodologies to identify trends, forecast performance, optimize processes, and contribute directly to informed decision-making.
